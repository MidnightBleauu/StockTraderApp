    Help Guide

    Here's a brief guide to get you started.

    Accounts Tab:

    • Click on 'Accounts' to view detailed information about your account, including balance, recent transactions, and available funds.
    • After reviewing your account details, you can navigate back to the dashboard by clicking 'Dashboard' in the top navigation bar.

    Portfolio Summary Tab:

    • To view an overview of your investments, click on 'Portfolio Summary'. This will show you key information, such as your portfolio's total value, daily changes, and performance.
    • Once you're done reviewing, you can click on 'Dashboard' to return to the main overview.

    Stock Portfolio Tab:

    • To access your stock portfolio, click on 'Stock Portfolio'. Here, you will see a list of stocks you currently hold, their prices, and performance over time.
    • Use the 'Back' button on the top navigation bar to return to the previous screen or select 'Dashboard' to go back to the main page.

    Navigate to Stock Page:

    • To view a specific stock's detailed performance, click on 'Stocks' in the top navigation bar. From here, search for the stock ticker in the search bar and hit 'Enter'.
    • You will then be directed to the stock page, where you can view real-time data, set price alerts, or execute a trade.

    Confirmation Messages:

    • After completing actions like setting a price alert or executing a trade, you will see confirmation messages like "Price Alert Set Successfully" or "Trade Executed Successfully", ensuring that each step is completed and users are aware of the outcome.

    Anytime you'd like to see this menu, click the "Help" button in your dashboard.
